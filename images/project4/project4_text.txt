WORK 4  Salmon Stocks  – a 1% for Art project Alaska Permanent Fund Corporation - Juneau Alaska 2021 Salmon Stocks  is a series of kiln formed solid art glass panels and free swimming fish representing Alaska King Salmon returns since 1972. Full size salmon are overlaid on an intricately styled water and graph background with all five of Alaska’s species represented; Chinook, Sockeye, Pink, Silver &amp; Chum in both their spawning and bright phases.
